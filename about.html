Who Are We?

We are a team of 4 passionate professionals with over 10 years of experience across various domains, including Software Development, Automation, Marketing, and Business Development. Based in Agartala, Tripura, a small yet beautiful state in North-East India. We bring a diverse set of skills to the table. Our journey together has been driven by a shared vision of simplifying digital solutions for businesses of all sizes.


When Did We Start brewnok?

brewnok was founded on 7th January 2025, with the aim to make digital solutions more accessible and user-friendly. We recognized the growing need for simplifying technology, and that's how brewnok was born.


Why Did We Start brewnok?

We observed that many digital solutions on the market were unnecessarily complex, often requiring a steep learning curve. For business owners, this complexity can become a major roadblock, wasting valuable time that could be better spent on growth and innovation. Recognizing this challenge, we saw an opportunity to create digital solutions that are simple, intuitive, and affordable. This initiative eventually led to the founding of brewnok. Our mission is to eliminate confusion and make technology work for businesses, not against them. We aim to deliver solutions that are efficient, effective, and straightforward - without the complexity that so often slows progress.


What's Our Mission?

In India, larger businesses are swiftly embracing digital transformation, while smaller businesses often struggle to keep up. Our mission at brewnok is to bridge this gap. We aim to provide top-notch, high-quality digital solutions at affordable rates for businesses of all sizes, enabling them to thrive in the digital age.

🎯 Our goal is simple: “to empower businesses, with the same level of technology that the industry giants provide, without the unnecessary complexity or hefty price tags.”
